Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Creator Studio collects and uses data. This is a template — update with your actual practices before publishing.

Data Collected

We collect contact form submissions (name, email, subject, message) and standard web analytics. Contact form data is sent to the site owner via SMTP and not stored on this public site unless explicitly saved.

Chatbot Data

Chat queries are processed server‑side to generate answers using site content. We recommend logging queries only if you need analytics; do not store sensitive personal data without consent.

Third Parties

Ad networks (e.g., AdSense) may collect data per their policies. Review and comply with their privacy requirements before enabling ads.
